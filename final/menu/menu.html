Menu

Our menu changes daily and seasonally but these are a few items we carry regularly. We make everything from scratch in small batches so our products do run out and availability changes all the time. If you are wanting something specific, please give us a call and we can let you know if we have it available at the time. All whole cakes and pies must be ordered at least 48 hours in advance.

*Nothing in our bakery is gluten free. We make some items that have no gluten ingredients but our kitchen is very small and cross contamination is always a possibility.
